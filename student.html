Eligibility Requirements

Check if you meet the necessary criteria for student migration

Academic Requirements

  • Completed secondary education
  • Minimum GPA requirements (varies by institution)
  • English language proficiency (IELTS/TOEFL)
  • Specific prerequisites for chosen program

Visa Requirements

  • Valid passport
  • Letter of acceptance from institution
  • Proof of financial support
  • Health insurance coverage
  • Medical examination (some countries)

Financial Requirements

  • Proof of tuition payment ability
  • Living expense funds (varies by country)
  • Bank statements (typically 3-6 months)
  • Scholarship documentation (if applicable)

Required Documents

Essential documents needed for your student migration application

Academic Documents

  • High school transcripts
  • Degree certificates (if applicable)
  • English proficiency test results
  • Standardized test scores (SAT, ACT, etc.)
  • Letters of recommendation

Personal Documents

  • Valid passport
  • Birth certificate
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Medical examination reports
  • Statement of purpose

Financial Documents

  • Bank statements
  • Sponsorship letter (if applicable)
  • Scholarship award letters
  • Proof of tuition payment
  • Proof of accommodation arrangements
