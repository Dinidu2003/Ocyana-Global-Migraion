Frequently Asked Questions
Find answers to common questions about our language and interview support services
What's the difference between IELTS and PTE?

IELTS (International English Language Testing System) and PTE (Pearson Test of English) are both English proficiency tests but differ in format. IELTS offers both paper-based and computer-based options, while PTE is fully computer-based. IELTS has a face-to-face speaking test, whereas PTE's speaking is recorded. PTE results are typically available faster (within 2-5 days) compared to IELTS (13 days). Some countries and institutions prefer one over the other, so it's important to check which test is accepted for your specific visa or application.

How long does it take to prepare for IELTS/PTE?

Preparation time varies based on your current English level and target score. On average: For a 0.5-1.0 band improvement: 4-6 weeks of intensive study. For a 1.0-1.5 band improvement: 8-12 weeks of regular study. For beginners needing significant improvement: 3-6 months. Our assessment test can give you a more accurate estimate based on your specific situation.

What common visa interview questions should I prepare for?

Common visa interview questions include: Why do you want to visit this country? What ties do you have to your home country? How will you fund your stay? What is your educational/professional background? Where will you stay during your visit? What are your plans after your visa expires? We provide comprehensive question banks and practice sessions tailored to your specific visa type.

Can you guarantee I'll pass my language test or interview?

While we can't guarantee results (as final outcomes depend on your effort and performance), our students have a 95% success rate in achieving their target scores or passing interviews on their first or second attempt. We provide proven strategies, personalized feedback, extensive practice materials, and realistic mock tests and interviews.

We offer flexible learning options to suit your needs: Online Live Classes with interactive sessions, In-Person Coaching at our certified partner centers, Self-Paced Learning with access to our online platform, and Hybrid Model combining live and self-paced learning. All options include personalized feedback, practice tests, and instructor support.
