Frequently Asked Questions
Find answers to common questions about contacting us
How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our emergency contact number for immediate assistance.

Do I need to book an appointment for a consultation?

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure you receive dedicated time with one of our migration specialists. You can book online or by phone.

What documents should I bring to my first consultation?

For your first consultation, please bring any identification documents, educational certificates, work experience proofs, and any previous visa applications if applicable. Our team will guide you on specific requirements based on your migration goals.

Do you offer remote consultations?

Yes, we offer comprehensive remote consultations via video call, phone, or email to accommodate clients who cannot visit our office in person.

Is there a fee for the initial consultation?

We offer a free 30-minute initial consultation to assess your situation and determine how we can assist you. For more in-depth consultations, a nominal fee may apply which can be credited toward any services you engage us for.
